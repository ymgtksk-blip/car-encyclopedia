Kia Cee'd 1.6 CRDi 115 (5-door hatchback)

First-gen Kia Cee'd (ED), a C-segment hatchback built in Slovakia for Europe (2006-2012). The 1.6 CRDi 115 diesel makes 85 kW/255 Nm, 0-100 in 11.5 s; Europe-only, not sold in Japan.
