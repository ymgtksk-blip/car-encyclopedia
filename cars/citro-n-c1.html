Citroën C1 1.0i

City car (2005–2014) co-developed by PSA Peugeot Citroën and Toyota and built in the Czech Republic; a triplet of the Toyota Aygo and Peugeot 107. The 1.0i has a 998cc 1KR-FE three-cylinder making 68 PS and 93 N·m, weighs 790 kg, and does 0–100 km/h in 13.7 s. Front-wheel drive; not officially sold in Japan.
