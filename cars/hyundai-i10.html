Hyundai i10 1.1 iRDE

First-gen Hyundai i10 (PA), an A-segment city hatchback built in Chennai, India. The 1.1 iRDE launch variant uses a 1086 cc SOHC inline-4 with 67 PS and 99 N·m, FWD via 5-speed manual. A practical, low-cost daily city car (2007-2013).
