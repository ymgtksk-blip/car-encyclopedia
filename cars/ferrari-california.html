Ferrari California (F149)

The 2008–2012 Ferrari California (F149) was Ferrari's first front-mid V8 GT and first with a folding metal hardtop. Its 4.3 L V8 makes 460 PS; 7-speed DCT, RWD, 0–100 km/h in 4.0 s.
