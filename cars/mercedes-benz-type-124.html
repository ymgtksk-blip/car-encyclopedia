Mercedes-Benz 300 E (W124)

W124 mid-size executive saloon (1985-1992). The 300 E pairs a 3.0L M103 inline-six (190 PS, non-catalyst) with front-engine RWD. Famed for over-engineered durability; now a modern classic.
