Subaru WRX STI

High-performance AWD sport sedan tuned by Subaru Tecnica International. The US-spec EJ257 2.5L turbo boxer makes about 309 PS and 393 N·m with a 6-speed manual. Rally-bred lineage from 1992, ended 2021.
