GAZ-69

Soviet 4x4 light utility off-roader from GAZ (from 1953), later built at UAZ until 1972; over 600,000 made. 2.11 L side-valve inline-four (55 PS, 125 Nm), 3-speed manual with 2-speed transfer case. Base is an 8-seat canvas-top; GAZ-69A a 5-seat 4-door. ~1525 kg, top speed ~90 km/h. License-built in Romania and North Korea.
