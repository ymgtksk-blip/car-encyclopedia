Sonata (first generation, Y1) 2.0

First-gen Hyundai Sonata (Y1, 1985-1988), the only rear-wheel-drive Sonata; an upmarket restyled Stellar with a 2.0 L Mitsubishi Sirius-derived I4 (110 PS). Sold poorly, replaced by the FWD Y2.
