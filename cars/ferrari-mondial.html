Ferrari Mondial

Mid-engined V8 2+2 grand tourer built by Ferrari in Italy from 1980 to 1993. The launch Mondial 8 used a transverse 2.9L 214 PS V8; later versions (QV, 3.2, t) grew to 3.4L and 300 PS.
