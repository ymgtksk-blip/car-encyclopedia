Audi Quattro (Ur-Quattro, Typ 85)

First car to pair a turbocharged front engine with Audi's permanent quattro four-wheel drive (1980). Original WR variant: 2,144 cc inline-5 turbo, 200 PS, 285 N·m, ~1,290 kg, 0-100 km/h in ~7.1 s, top speed ~220 km/h. Revolutionized sports cars and Group B rallying.
