Peugeot 107 1.0 (3-door, 5-speed manual)

City car (2005-2014) from PSA's B-Zero joint venture with Toyota; a triplet of the Citroen C1 and Toyota Aygo, built in Kolin, Czech Republic. The 1.0 3-door 5-speed manual uses Toyota's 998 cc 1KR-FE inline-3 with 68 PS and 93 Nm, weighs about 790 kg, and reaches 0-100 km/h in 13.7 s. Never officially sold in Japan.
