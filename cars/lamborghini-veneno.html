Lamborghini Veneno LP 750-4 (Coupé)

Aventador-based limited-edition hypercar for Lamborghini's 50th anniversary (2013). Coupé: 3 customer cars plus 1 prototype. NA 6.5L V12, 750 PS, 690 N·m, AWD, 7-speed ISR; 0-100 km/h in 2.8 s, ~355 km/h. The 1,450 kg figure is dry weight.
