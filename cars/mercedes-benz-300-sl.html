Mercedes-Benz 300 SL "Gullwing" (W198)

Mercedes-Benz 300 SL 'Gullwing' (W198): 1954-57 two-seat sports coupe. First production car with mechanical direct fuel injection; 3.0L inline-six, 215 PS, tubular spaceframe with iconic upward gullwing doors. Widely seen as the first modern supercar.
