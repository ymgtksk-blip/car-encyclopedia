Porsche Cayman (981)

Second-generation (981) Porsche Cayman, a mid-engine rear-wheel-drive sports coupé. The base 2.7-litre flat-six makes 275 PS and 290 N·m, reaching 0-100 km/h in 5.7 s. Built 2013-2016.
