Volkswagen up! 1.0 MPI 60 PS

The Volkswagen up! is an A-segment (city) car launched at the September 2011 Frankfurt IAA, with European sales from December 2011. This entry petrol variant uses a naturally aspirated 999 cc inline-3 (1.0 MPI) producing 60 PS (44 kW) at ~5,000-5,500 rpm and 95 N·m from 3,000 rpm, driving the front wheels via a 5-speed manual. Kerb weight from 929 kg gives 0-100 km/h in 14.4 s and a top speed of about 160 km/h. Built mainly in Bratislava, Slovakia, the up! also underpinned the related SEAT Mii and Skoda Citigo.
