Maserati Quattroporte

Italian full-size luxury sedan built by Maserati from 1963 to 2023. The sixth-generation (M156) GTS flagship uses a 3.8L twin-turbo V8 with 530 PS and 650 N·m, rear-wheel drive, 0-100 km/h in 4.7s.
