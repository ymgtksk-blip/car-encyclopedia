Ford LTD Crown Victoria LX (5.0L V8)

The 1991 LTD Crown Victoria was the last year of Ford's original boxy, body-on-frame full-size sedan on the rear-drive Panther platform. The LX 4-door used a 5.0L EFI V8 (150 hp net, ≈152 PS; 270 lb-ft, ≈366 N·m) with a 4-speed AOD automatic, RWD; curb weight ≈1,734 kg. Redesigned with aero styling for 1992.
