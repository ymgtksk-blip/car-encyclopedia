Peugeot 3008 1.6 THP 156

First-gen Peugeot 3008 (T84, 2009), a compact crossover/MPV on PSA's PF2 platform. Uses the BMW-co-developed 1.6L EP6DT turbo four (1598 cc), 156 PS and 240 N·m from 1400 rpm, front-wheel drive. 0-100 km/h in 8.9 s (manual), ~202 km/h top, base kerb ~1459 kg. DIN-net figures. Gen-1 ran until replaced in 2016.
