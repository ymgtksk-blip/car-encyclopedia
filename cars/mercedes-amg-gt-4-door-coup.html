Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupé

Germany's high-performance four-door (X290), launched in 2018. The flagship GT 63 S uses a 4.0L twin-turbo V8 with 639 PS and 900 Nm, 4MATIC+ AWD, reaching 100 km/h in 3.2 s.
