Opel Agila 1.2 16V (A)

The Opel Agila A is a tall mini-MPV / city car launched in 2000, badge-engineered from the Suzuki Wagon R+. This 1.2 16V uses GM's 1,199 cc Z12XE DOHC four making 75 PS at 5,600 rpm and 110 N·m at 4,000 rpm, driving the front wheels via a 5-speed manual for 0-100 km/h in about 13.5 s and 155 km/h top speed. Kerb-weight sources diverge widely, so it is left unconfirmed.
