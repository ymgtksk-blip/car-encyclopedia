Ford Edge

First-generation Ford Edge mid-size unibody crossover (2007). Pre-facelift SEL FWD with a 3.5L Duratec 35 V6: 265 hp (~269 PS), 339 Nm, 6-speed automatic. North American market, not sold in Japan.
