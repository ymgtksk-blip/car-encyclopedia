Duster 1.5 dCi 110 FAP 4x4 (Phase 1)

Budget compact SUV from Romania's Dacia (badged Renault Duster abroad), 2010-2018, on the low-cost B0 platform. Phase 1 1.5 dCi 110 FAP 4x4: 1,461 cc turbo-diesel, 109 PS, 240 N·m, on-demand AWD, 1,294 kg, 0-100 km/h in 12.5 s.
