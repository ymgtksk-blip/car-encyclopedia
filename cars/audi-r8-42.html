Audi R8 (Type 42)

First-generation Audi R8 mid-engine sports car (2006-2015). Launch 4.2 FSI V8 made 420 PS and 430 Nm, quattro AWD, 0-100 km/h in 4.6 s; a 5.2 V10 was added later.
