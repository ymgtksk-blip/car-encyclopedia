Volkswagen Touran 1.6 FSI (Typ 1T, first generation)

First-generation compact MPV (Typ 1T), VW's first on the Golf Mk5 (PQ35) platform. The 1.6 FSI: a 1,598 cc direct-injection inline-4 (EA111) making 116 PS and 155 N.m, with a 6-speed manual and front-wheel drive. 0-100 km/h in 11.9 s; 186 km/h top speed; kerb weight about 1,423 kg.
