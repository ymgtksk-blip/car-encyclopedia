Peugeot 607 3.0 V6 24V

France's executive flagship saloon (1999-2004), replacing the 605. Phase 1 3.0 V6 24V (ES9J4, 207 PS DIN at 6000 rpm, 285 N·m), 5-speed manual, FWD; 0-100 km/h in 8.1 s. Marketed as 'V6 210' (a designation, not DIN power).
