Audi TT Coupé 1.8 T quattro 180 PS

First-generation Audi TT (Type 8N), a 2+2 sports coupé built 1998-2006 in Győr. The launch 1.8 T quattro pairs a 180 PS turbo inline-4 with Haldex AWD and a 5-speed manual.
