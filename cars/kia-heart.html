Kia Picanto (New Morning, KR)

Korean-market first-gen Kia Picanto (SA), the 2008 'New Morning' facelift. Launch 1.0 L (999 cc) Epsilon SOHC inline-4 petrol, 5-speed manual, FWD 5-door city hatch: 64 PS, 86 N·m, 877 kg. Counted as a Korean 경차 light car after the 2008 1,000 cc limit raise, not a Japanese 660 cc kei. No factory 'Heart' trim, 0-100 time or Japanese price confirmed.
