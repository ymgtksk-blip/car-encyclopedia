Lexus RX 300 (XU10)

First-gen Lexus RX 300 (XU10) is credited with creating the modern luxury crossover SUV: a car-based unibody rather than a body-on-frame off-roader. Exported from 1998 on the Toyota Harrier platform (launched Dec 1997). This NA RX 300 AWD (MCU15) uses a 3.0 L 1MZ-FE VVT-i V6 with a 4-speed auto and Snow mode, prioritizing quiet, easy comfort. Europe's RX 300 was rated 201 PS; figures here are the NA SAE-net variant.
