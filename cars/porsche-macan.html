Porsche Macan

Compact luxury performance SUV by Porsche, built in Leipzig from 2014. The Macan S has a 2,997 cc twin-turbo V6 (340 PS, 460 N·m), AWD with 7-speed PDK, 0-100 km/h in 5.2 s.
