190 E 2.0 (W201)

Fuel-injected base version of Mercedes' first compact "Baby Benz" (W201). 2.0L M102 inline-4, 122 PS, 175 N·m, RWD, 5-speed, 0-100 in ~10.9 s. Multi-link rear, built 1982-1993.
