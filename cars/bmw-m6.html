BMW M6

BMW's high-performance grand tourer based on the 6 Series, built 1983–2019 across three generations. The final F12/F13 packs a 4.4L twin-turbo V8: 560 PS, 680 Nm, RWD, 0–100 km/h in 4.2 s.
