Nissan GT-R (R35, 2011 facelift)

First facelift of the R35 GT-R (DBA-R35). Its 3.8L VR38DETT twin-turbo V6 makes 530 PS and 612 N·m, driving all four wheels via a dual-clutch gearbox.
