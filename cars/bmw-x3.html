BMW X3 3.0i (E83)

First-gen BMW X3 (E83), BMW's first compact luxury SUV. Pre-facelift 3.0i with M54 3.0L NA inline-six: 231 PS, 300 Nm, xDrive AWD, 0-100 in 7.8 s. In Japan from mid-2004, automatic-only, 5,730,000 yen.
