McLaren F1

British mid-engined three-seat hypercar (1992–1998). Carbon monocoque, 6.1L BMW S70/2 V12, 627 PS, 650 N·m, 1,138 kg dry, 0–100 km/h ~3.2 s. Only 64 road cars; set a 386 km/h production-car speed record in 1998.
