Yugo 45 (Zastava Koral 45)

Entry-level 903cc launch variant of Zastava's subcompact 3-door hatchback, built in Kragujevac from 1980. FF, OHV inline-four, 45 PS DIN, 62 N·m, 750 kg. RS is the country code, not a sporting trim.
