Audi A5 3.2 FSI quattro Coupe

First-generation Audi A5 (Typ 8T), launched 2007. The flagship 3.2 FSI quattro coupe pairs a 3.2L V6 (265 PS, 330 Nm) with quattro AWD; 0-100 km/h in 6.1 s, limited to 250 km/h.
