Mitsubishi Triton / L200 (Forte)

First-generation Mitsubishi pickup, launched in Japan in September 1978 as the 'Forte' and sold worldwide as the L200. Rear-wheel-drive 1.6L 4G32 single-cab; direct ancestor of today's Triton. 4WD added in 1980.
